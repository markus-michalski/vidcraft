# HeyGen Best Practices

## Script Optimization

- Keep each scene under 1500 characters
- Use natural pauses: commas and periods create slight pauses
- Avoid special characters that may be mispronounced
- Spell out acronyms on first use: "API, that is Application Programming Interface"
- Numbers: write "one hundred" not "100" for narration

## Avatar Selection

- Match avatar to target audience demographics
- Use consistent avatar across an entire series
- Choose avatars tested with your target language
- Professional appearance for B2B, casual for B2C/developer content

## Background

- Solid colors work best for professional content
- Avoid busy backgrounds that distract from the avatar
- Use branded backgrounds for series consistency
- Consider screen share layouts for tutorials

## Voice Settings

- Default speed (1.0x) works for most content
- Slow down to 0.9x for complex technical content
- Speed up to 1.1x for energetic marketing content
- Test the voice with your specific script before full generation

## Scene Structure

- One topic per scene
- Max 45 seconds per scene for viewer attention
- Use transitions between major topic changes
- Add pauses between sections (empty line in script)

## Common Issues

- Long sentences cause unnatural pacing → keep under 20 words
- Technical terms may be mispronounced → test and adjust spelling
- URLs should be spelled out or shown as on-screen text, not narrated
- Numbers over 1000 should use words, not digits
